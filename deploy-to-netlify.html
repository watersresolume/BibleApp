Step 2: Prepare Your Files

Make sure you have these files in your Downloads folder:

index.html
styles.css
app.js
bible-data.js
test.html
test-suite.js
package.json

Step 3: Drag and Drop

On the Netlify page:

  1. Drag your entire Downloads folder to the drop zone
  2. Wait for the upload to complete
  3. Netlify will automatically deploy your app
  4. You'll get a URL like: https://random-name.netlify.app

Step 4: Access on Your TV

Once deployed:

  1. Copy the Netlify URL
  2. Open your TV's web browser
  3. Paste the URL and press Enter
  4. Your Bible app will load immediately!
